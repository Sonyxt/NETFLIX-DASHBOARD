# NETFLIX-DASHBOARD

Project 

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Netflix content data to uncover insights related to content distribution, ratings, release trends, duration, and awards.
The objective is to understand Netflix’s content strategy and identify patterns in movies and series over time.

^^WHAT IT FEATURES!!!!

1️ Genre Filter (Interactive)

Dynamic slicer to filter content by genre (e.g., Romance, Action, Comedy, etc.)
Enables targeted analysis

2️ Show Duration Analysis

Distribution across:
1–2 hour content (Majority ~59.86%)
Less than 30 minutes
30–60 minutes
2+ hours
Insight: Netflix content is heavily dominated by standard movie-length duration.

3️ Yearly Release Trend

Comparison of Movies vs Series by year
Strong growth trend from 2016–2020
Movies consistently higher than Series

4️ Awards Received by Shows

Movies receive significantly more awards than Series
Peak award trend around 2020

5️ View Rating Distribution

Breakdown by:
R
PG-13
Not Rated
TV-MA (highest share ~37.84%)
TV-14
Others
Insight: Mature content (TV-MA) dominates the platform.

6️ Searchable Show List

Search bar to quickly find titles
Improves usability for stakeholders
